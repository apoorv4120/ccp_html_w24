Pets


Scotch

Scotch is a 3 year old Shiba Inu. I adopted him as a pup from one of my friends, and ever since then he's a part of our little family.

He is the most friendly dog I've ever seen. He instantly warms up to any guest and loves meeting new people while on a walk.

Likes

  • Belly rubs
  • Getting his pictures taken
  • Playing with his toys


Luna

Luna is a 1 month old kitten we adopted a couple of days ago. She is named after 'Luna Lovegood' from Harry Potter (named by my sister) She is the cutest creature on the planet.

Luna, the adventurous kitten, once turned a mundane day into a spectacle by fearlessly scaling a towering bookshelf, leaving a trail of chaos and curious smiles in her tiny, mischievous wake.

Likes

  • Sleeping
  • Eating
  • Playing with her rope


Yappy

Yappy is a 2 year old parrot. The name Yappy was kept by my father since he keeps yapping the entire day.

Yappy, the spirited parrot, stole the spotlight at a family gathering with his uncanny ability to mimic everyone's laughter, turning a quiet evening into a riot of contagious joy.

Likes

  • Mimicing guests
  • Singing
  • Flying around the house
